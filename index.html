Band Bio

The Unaffected is a high-energy musical collective that fearlessly blends the raw power of hard rock and metal with the catchy melodies of pop rock, the soulful depths of blues, and the rebellious spirit of grunge.

Born from a shared passion for diverse sounds, the band has cultivated a unique sonic identity that defies categorization. Called The Unaffected, they are a rock band from Salt Lake City.
